You're in. Welcome to the Lead Engine.

A receipt is on its way to your email. Here's how to get running in about five minutes.

1Download the plugin

This file is your licensed copy — keep it safe:

Download ringy-lead-engine.plugin

2Install it in Cowork

Open the Claude desktop app, go to Cowork, and drag the ringy-lead-engine.plugin file into the chat to install it.

3Run the setup wizard

Just say: "Set up the lead engine." It'll ask for your assistant's name, your name and company, your cell and timezone, connect your Calendly, read your Ringy account to map your dispositions, and create your scheduled jobs. Nothing sends until you approve.

Need a hand? Reply to your receipt email. Remember: you're responsible for having consent to text your leads and for following SMS rules in your area.
